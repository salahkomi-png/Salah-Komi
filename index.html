Why Dogs are the Best Pets

Introduction

Dogs are known for their loyalty, intelligence, and companionship. They have been part of human life for thousands of years, serving as hunters, workers, and beloved pets. There is a reason they are often called "man's best friend"!

Some Fun Facts About Dogs

  • Dogs have an extraordinary sense of smell, up to 100,000 times more sensitive than humans.
  • The Basenji dog is the only breed that doesn't bark.
  • Dogs can understand up to 165 words and signals.
